C:\WHERE_THIS_MODEL_FAILS.TXT
Large-world forces this small-world model doesn't see
  • Sentiment & attention: no Reddit r/pkmntcg volume, Google Trends, or Twitter mention velocity. Demand-surge buckets are structurally under-specified here.
  • Cultural catalysts: no anime episode airings, no set-release hype curves, no tournament top-8 decklists, no influencer/artist collab announcements.
  • Reprint risk: modeled heuristically from era + rarity, not from set-release calendar or TPCi reprint signals.
  • Grading supply shocks: PSA population changes are lagged; sudden pop-report drops aren't caught in real-time.
  • Macro/whale moves: crypto-correlated liquidity, whale wallet flows, and macro risk-on/off regime are not inputs.
This section exists because calibrated trust > maximum trust. The UI should teach you where the model is weak, not only where it's strong. Source: Google PAIR Guidebook.
